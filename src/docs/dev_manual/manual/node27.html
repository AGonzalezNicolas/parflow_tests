7. Gridding

The Applied Mathematics Group has extensive experience with adaptive mesh refinement techniques, or AMR. In an effort to utilize this knowledge, and keep coherent the various efforts within the CCSE, the gridding within is a parallel adaptation of AMR. The differences between the two approaches are mostly a consequence of parallel computing.

See also, 11.1.
