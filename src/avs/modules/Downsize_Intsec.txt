NAME
	Downsize_Intsec

SUMMARY
	Name	Downsize_Intsec

	Type	Filter

	Inputs	
        (1) PF_field_1, (3D 3-space scalar rectilinear float OPTIONAL)
	  Optional ParFlow input, Vertical scale will be expanded.
        (2) PF_field_2, (3D 3-space scalar rectilinear float OPTIONAL)
	  Optional ParFlow input, Vertical scale will be expanded.
        (3) X_Inter_by_yz, (3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float REQUIRED)
	  Projection (X values) of yz grid on surfaces
        (4) Y_Inter_by_zx, (3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float REQUIRED)
	  Projection (Y values) of zx grid on surfaces
        (5) Z_Inter_by_xy, (3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float REQUIRED)
	  Projection (Z values) of xy grid on surfaces

	Outputs	
        (1) dn_PF_field_1, (3D 3-space scalar rectilinear float)
	  Optional ParFlow output, downsized & vertically expanded. 
        (2)dn_ PF_field_2, (3D 3-space scalar rectilinear float)
	  Optional ParFlow output, downsized & vertically expanded.
        (3) dn_X_Inter_by_yz, (3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float)
	  Projection (X values) of yz grid on surfaces, Downsized & 
          vertically expanded.
        (4) dn_Y_Inter_by_zx, (3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float)
	  Projection (Y values) of zx grid on surfaces, Downsized & 
          vertically expanded.
        (5) dn_Z_Inter_by_xy, (3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float)
	  Projection (Z values) of xy grid on surfaces, Downsized & 
          vertically expanded.

        Parameters  
         (1) Downsize, down size ratio.
         (2) Z_expand, factor used to expand z grid and z intersections.

DESCRIPTION
	Downsizes the two coordinate dimensions of the three intesection data fields,
        X_Inter_by_yz, Y_Inter_by_zx and Z_Inter_by_xy, (these fields are 3d, however,
        only two dimensions are coordinates) also downsizes two
        optional rectilinear ParFlow fields, PF_field_1 and PF_field_2
        Module also does vertical expansion of all z coordinate values and z
        intersection data.
