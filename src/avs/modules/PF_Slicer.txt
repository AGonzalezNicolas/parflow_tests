NAME
	PF_Slicer

SUMMARY
	Name	PF_Slicer

	Type	Mapper

	Inputs	
          (1) PF_field_in 3D 3-space scalar rectilinear float", REQUIRED
          (2) X_inter_by_yz 3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float", REQUIRED
          (3) Y_inter_by_zx 3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float", REQUIRED
          (4) Z_inter_by_xy 3D 3-space 2-vector rectilinear float", REQUIRED

        Output
	  (1) Slice_out, geom

        Parameter 
          (1) Slice_Axis, Direction of slice plane
	  (2) Slice_plane, slice plane value (corrdinate perpendicluar to slice plane)
          (3) Starting index of "first" coordinates in slice plane.
          (4) Finishing index of "first" coordinates in slice plane.
          (5) Starting index of "second" coordinates in slice plane.
          (6) Finishing index of "second"coordinates in slice plane.
          (7) Solid_num, the solid number used to bound the slice plane. 

DESCRIPTION
          Draws a slice plane, (x,y,or z) bounded by the picked geo unit (Solid_num).

Note: Both finishing index, (parameter #4 and #6 above) are made
      invisible in the current version.
